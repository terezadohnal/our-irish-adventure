Our irish adventure

One Year on an Island. Two People trying to get the most of it.

How the time went..

Cliffs of Moher

First time meeting the giant Cliffs. The Cliffs of Moher. Covered by a greeny land, surrounded by the wild Atlantic Ocean. Unbelievable height. Neverending walk along the coast. Sunlight on my back. Butterflies in my stomach.

Welcome to Kerry

The beautiful county Kerry. Where the grass is greener, cows are darker, lands are larger, sunsets are greater and emotions are stronger. Each corner of this Kerry Ring is mysteriously stunning.

Streets of Limerick

Firstly, the Limerick streets seemed to be very dark and lonely. My stomach started to ache and vision of an upcomming year was fading away. Then there was Rift. The coffeeshop. Which made our stay in Ireland so special and unique.

Killarney

Wide and empty land. Huge national parc with a speacial atmosphere. Some places looks better when the sun clouds come out. You can see the nature clearly and not blinded by the sun. The river gets darker and grass gets greener.
